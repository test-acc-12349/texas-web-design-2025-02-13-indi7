About Texas Web Design

Founded in 2015 by a team of passionate developers and designers, Texas Web Design emerged from a simple vision: to provide Texas businesses with world-class websites at reasonable prices. What started as a small studio in Austin has grown into a leading digital agency serving clients across the Lone Star State.

Our mission is to empower Texas businesses with powerful digital solutions that drive growth and success. We believe in combining cutting-edge technology with exceptional design to create websites that not only look stunning but also deliver measurable results. Our core values of transparency, innovation, and client success guide everything we do.

Frequently Asked Questions

What's included in your web design packages?

Our comprehensive packages include custom design, responsive development, SEO optimization, free hosting, SSL certification, and ongoing support. Every project also includes detailed analytics setup and basic SEO optimization.

How long does the design process take?

Most projects are completed within 4-6 weeks, depending on complexity. We follow a structured timeline: discovery (1 week), design (2 weeks), development (2 weeks), and testing/launch (1 week).

Do you offer ongoing maintenance and updates?

Yes, we provide comprehensive maintenance packages including regular updates, security monitoring, content updates, and technical support. Our team is available during business hours for immediate assistance.

What makes your hosting service different?

Our enterprise-grade hosting includes daily backups, DDoS protection, global CDN, and 99.9% uptime guarantee. We manage all technical aspects, allowing you to focus on your business.

Yes, we offer professional copywriting and content creation services. Our team can help develop engaging, SEO-optimized content that resonates with your target audience.
